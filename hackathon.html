Lack of awareness about the farming schemes introduced by the Governement is one of the major reasons for the plight of the farmers in our country today.Instead of worrying about the reasons, it's important for us to understand that it is the responsibility of everyone to play their part in spreading the awarneness among the farmers.
Agritech is one such initiative which provides all the information regarding the most important farming laws and schemes that the Government has introduced till date. We also provide information for all the enthusiasists out there who are genuinely intrested in Indian farming. Agritech can be the main tool for every farmer who want know the laws and schemes brought for them in our country.
